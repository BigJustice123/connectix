About Glowline

Bridging the gap between NYC’s vibrant local businesses and top-tier international marketing experts, Glowline empowers your brand to thrive in a global market — while staying true to your community’s unique spirit.

Our Story

As a local New Yorker, I witnessed firsthand how many small businesses struggled to keep pace with modern marketing trends. Despite incredible products and services, they often fell behind due to lack of resources, expertise, or time. Glowline was born from this insight — to connect these passionate businesses with world-class marketing professionals, blending local knowledge with international expertise to elevate every brand’s potential.

Our First Client: Laura

Laura, the creative mind behind Flowers by Valli, is a passionate florist serving New York since 1970. She has built a beautiful business offering fresh flowers, house plants, and unique gifts that bring joy to every occasion. Partnering with Laura on her marketing journey inspired Glowline to support more local businesses in capturing their essence while reaching broader audiences.
